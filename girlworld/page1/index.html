the world used to be mine ... running and

shouting and dreaming in the backyard of my

childhood home. hours spent grasping at what

it meant to miss something i never had ...

i still spend too much time thinking about

it, about all the things i couldn't

understand at 8 year old, except now the

world does not belong to me ... i am

restless yet, nowhere to run or shout,

though every once in a while i take for

myself a slice of world just big enough to

dream ... i take up space in the shower,

staring out the window at all of the lonely

rooftops, i take up space on the edge of

the bathtub in a towel as i wait for the

fog on the mirror to clear, i take up

space on the toilet pissing and shitting and

remembering how it felt to walk outside to

the whole wide world that belonged only to

me
